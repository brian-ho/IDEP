The Image of the City, redux

This book is about the look of cities, and whether this look is of any importance, and whether it can be changed. The urban landscape, among its many roles, is also something to be seen, to be remembered, and to delight in. Giving visual forn to the city is a special kind of design problem, and a rather new one at that.

Brian Ho - Harvard GSD and SEAS, MDE 2018
